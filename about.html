Artist Bio

Born from paint-stained hands, torch burns, many slivers, and the hum of too many creative experiments at once, The Jack is not just a name, it's a manifesto. Aiden Beard is a self-taught, detail-driven artist who builds beautiful things from whatever's available. With hands that have touched everything from glass to graphite to epoxy and digital design, Aiden’s practice is defined by experimentation, resilience, and raw creativity. Aiden Beard is a multidisciplinary artist and designer pursuing a degree in Media Arts and Sciences with a focus on graphic design and animation. His work spans hand-drawn pieces, digital design, wood and epoxy, and custom commissions. The result is an ever-evolving portfolio shaped by curiosity and the necessity to create, with a creative process that often begins with what’s available and ends with something unforgettable. Every project, from client commissions to personal experiments, is approached with thoughtful intention and a drive for lasting impact. Aiden’s strengths lie in creative problem-solving, attention to fine detail, and the ability to turn even modest materials into pieces that resonate.
