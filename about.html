Artist Bio

Born from paint-stained hands, soldering burns, and the hum of too many creative experiments at once, The Jack is not just a name — it's a manifesto. I work in wood, thread, ink, pixels, and flame. Every piece is a story carved, stitched, burned, and built. This space exists to show the work and the weird magic that happens between the idea and the artifact.

Aiden Beard
